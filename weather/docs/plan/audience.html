Personas

Persona #1: Barry White is a rexburg local currently serving as a bishop in a BYUI ward. He is 83 years old. This is Barry's 17th time as bishop and he loves to take the students hiking. He is using an old desktop computer. Barry is a married man.

Persona #2: Erika Blake is an 18 year old freshman student at BYUI. She and her roomates love to go on adventures, weather permitting. Erika uses her Iphone X to check the weather every day.

Scenarios

Scenario 2: Jared is unsure which weekend he should plan a trip home to go see his parents. He checks the weather reports to see when the best time to drive would be.


Scenario 3: Lucy is going on a camping trip. She checks the weather to see if she should pack rain clothes or not.


Scenario 4: Greg is a farmer. He regualrly is checking the weather to see if there is going to be an early frost. He has to harvest before it freezes over.


Scenario 5: Christian is thinking about moving to rexburg. He decided to check out the tempurature in rexburg online to help him make his final decision. Christian decided to move to Arizona.
